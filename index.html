Empowering dreams across borders, bridging talents with opportunities, Alaqsha Manpower Consultancy gives wings to aspirations, building a future without limits.

For years, Alaqsha Manpower Consultancy has been committed to sourcing and providing skilled and unskilled workers to meet the growing demands of industries across the Gulf and beyond. We specialize in matching the right talent to the right opportunity, ensuring that businesses thrive and individuals find meaningful, life-changing employment.

Our services extend across industries such as construction, hospitality, healthcare, and engineering, with a track record of success built on trust, reliability, and efficiency. At Alaqsha, we believe in empowering people and contributing to the success of global enterprises.

Industries we Serve

Providing our clients with the best of our capabilities.

Construction Manpower

We specialize in providing skilled and unskilled labor for the construction and engineering sectors, including roles such as civil engineers, site supervisors, carpenters, welders, and masons. Our workers are well-trained to meet the rigorous demands of large-scale projects in the Gulf and beyond, ensuring timely project completion and quality standards.

Medical / Para-Medical

Alaqsha connects healthcare institutions with highly skilled doctors, nurses, technicians, and support staff. Our thorough screening process ensures that we provide only the best medical professionals, contributing to the success of hospitals and clinics in the Middle East.

Hospitality

From hotels to restaurants, we supply qualified hospitality professionals to cater to the growing demands of the service industry. Whether it’s chefs, hotel managers, waitstaff, or housekeeping personnel, we ensure that our candidates bring professionalism, efficiency, and excellence to every role they take on.

Management Industry

Our administrative recruitment services connect businesses with experienced clerical and office support staff, including secretaries, receptionists, HR personnel, and data entry operators. These professionals bring organizational skills, efficiency, and reliability to help businesses run smoothly.

Skilled / Unskilled Labour

Alaqsha provides a wide range of skilled and semi-skilled workers for industries such as construction, manufacturing, and infrastructure development. From welders and electricians to general laborers, we ensure our workforce meets your project’s needs and standards.

Oil & Gas Industry (Petrochemical)

We provide manpower solutions for industrial and technical roles, including mechanics, electricians, technicians, and equipment operators. With a strong focus on safety and technical expertise, our candidates are ready to support projects in heavy industries, including oil and gas, manufacturing, and petrochemicals.
